Hello, there! My name is Kat Terranova. I have been a frontend engineer for over 6 years, and I have finally decided to rip the bandaid off and make a technical blog. To be honest, I am **very** new to this... so new in fact, I am  historically a notorious lurker in all forms of social media; Facebook, Insta, Reddit, you name it. One of the main reasons could be that someone out there will finally learn that I am an imposter and I actually have no idea what I'm doing... but sources say I am not the only one who feels this way. So this is my way of overcoming this self made obstacle and embracing my stupidity! 

Now that we have that over-explained and awkward introduction out of the way, this blog will contain mostly technical topics: Frontend Stacks, Technical News, the big buzz of AI, and maybe a sprinkle of digital art and photography sprinkled in there. My goal for this blog is to improve my writing skills, share my knowledge from projects and problems I have overcome, and to also look back on things that I have worked on in the past. I find that a lot of times things can get pretty busy and you can sometimes forget the things that you overcome when you are constantly moving on to the next project or ticket. 

With all the hustle and bustle of AI, I am of course using AI everyday for work. I even used AI to create this site to showcase my personal work and house this blog. However, my number 1 rule for this blog is that I am not going to use AI to write any of my posts since this blog is a way for my to work on my own writing, I feel like that wouldn't be fair to myself. I know that this definately sounds easier said than done, but I will try my best to keep writing once a month (to start).
